Our Programs



Playgroup

As per RTE- Right to Education and NEP- National Education Policy from state to state.

Children in this age group are identified as Toddlers. Our Playgroup curriculum is designed to nurture curiosity, independence, and social skills through exploration and play.

  • Promoting positive peer social interaction
  • Opportunities for basic motor development
  • Math Readiness (Number Recognition)
  • Language Readiness (Letter Recognition)
  • Exploration-based learning
Special Activities:
  • Artsy, Personality Development, Showstopper, Mental Might
  • Augmented Reality - Fantasy Box, Eco-conscious, Tell-a-Tale, Library
  • Sciencify, Whirl & Twirl, Sensorium, Critical Thinking - Problem Solving, Integrated Sanskar
Daily Sessions:
  • Circle Time, Free Play, Knowledge Time, Language Time, Numeracy Time, Talk Time
  • Outdoor & Indoor Play, Celebration of festivals and special days, Field Trips, Puppet shows and skits

Nursery

As per RTE and NEP guidelines, the Nursery program is tailored for early learners, focusing on foundational skills and joyful discovery.

Children in this age group begin to develop independence and a love for learning. The curriculum encourages language, cognitive, and physical development through structured and free play.

  • Social and emotional development
  • Fine and gross motor skills
  • Pre-writing and pre-math concepts
  • Language development (phonics, vocabulary)
  • Creative expression through art and music
Special Activities:
  • Storytelling and rhymes, Sand and water play, Nature walks and exploration
  • Sensory activities, Art & Craft, Music & Movement, Role play and dramatics, Library time
Daily Sessions:
  • Circle Time, Theme-based learning, Language and Math readiness, Free Play, Outdoor activities
  • Celebration of festivals and special days, Field Trips, Puppet shows and skits

Junior KG

Aligned with NEP and RTE, Junior KG focuses on building strong academic and social foundations for preschoolers.

Children in this group are introduced to early literacy and numeracy, critical thinking, and collaborative play. The curriculum is designed to foster curiosity, confidence, and creativity.

  • Letter and number recognition and formation
  • Phonics and early reading skills
  • Counting, sorting, and patterning
  • Science and discovery activities
  • Social skills and teamwork
Special Activities:
  • Art & Craft, Music & Dance, Storytelling and dramatics, Show and Tell
  • Environmental awareness, Simple science experiments, Library time
Daily Sessions:
  • Circle Time, Language and Math sessions, Knowledge Time, Free Play, Outdoor games and sports
  • Celebration of festivals and special days, Field Trips, Puppet shows and skits

Senior KG

Senior KG prepares children for formal schooling, focusing on advanced concepts, independence, and holistic development.

The curriculum emphasizes readiness for Grade 1, with a balance of academics, life skills, and creative pursuits.

  • Reading and writing simple sentences
  • Advanced numeracy (addition, subtraction, problem-solving)
  • Science and environmental studies
  • Social studies and general awareness
  • Value education and life skills
Special Activities:
  • Public speaking and recitation, Art & Craft, Music & Dance, Project work and group activities
  • Science experiments, Library and reading time
Daily Sessions:
  • Circle Time, Language, Math, and Science sessions, Knowledge Time, Free Play, Outdoor sports and games
  • Celebration of festivals and special days, Field Trips, Puppet shows and skits
